ScroogeFrog is a full-cycle media buying agency since 2007, with a specialization in advertising traffic

  • We have been helping to attract customers by native and social media advertising
  • International and local traffic sources, turnkey work from creative to traffic tracking with optimization for results
  • We are practitioners and use up-to-date data sources as well as regularly expand the database of new sources

Who are our services suitable for?

  • Web and mobile projects

    We offer non-standard and local traffic sources and we help with end-to-end analytics

  • E-commerce business

    We optimize advertising traffic for KPI, work as a single window for different traffic sources, make custom reports

  • Media buying teams

    We help with payment, moderation and give agency accounts

  • BTL brand departments

    We comply with brand safety and work under international agreements

Stages of work

  • Step 1

    Initial consultation

    We discuss the work plan and the desired result, as well as geo, budget, KPI, landing pages, coordination of advertising sources

  • Step 2

    Concluding a contract

    We discuss flexible payment methods and sign an agreement

  • Step 3

    Preparation of advertising campaigns

    We create advertising accounts, ready-made creatives, set up analytics or CRM data collection, coordinate traffic markup

  • Step 4

    Launch and optimization

    Full control over advertising campaigns by our specialists, real-time optimization

  • Step 5

    The final stage

    We scale the results and, if necessary, add new sources, make test advertising campaigns

  • Advertising campaigns in native networks

    • we are an authorized partner of many native networks
    • we work with all currencies of the world, for the convenience of replenishment

    • we make individual accounts for the client
